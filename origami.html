Bioinspired Salp Robot Using Origami Structures

The salp (salpidae) is a gelatinous marine invertebrate related to jellyfish. They move through the water using a propulsion system where they contract and relax muscle bands that ring their bodies. Inspired by these creatures, this project aimed to replicate this kind of movement with the bistable snapping of Kresling tubes to create an efficient model water propulsion system for robotic applications.
